Privacy Policy

Your agricultural data security is our top priority. Learn how BananaGuard AI collects, processes, and protects your crop images and personal information.

Effective Date: May 23, 2026
1. Overview

At BananaGuard AI, we build technology for sustainable farming. We are committed to transparency in our data practices. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information collected through our crop diagnostic application, particularly concerning banana leaf disease detection.

2. Crop Image Collection & Processing

To diagnose banana leaf diseases using our deep neural network model (MobileNetV2), you must capture or upload images of banana plant leaves. Here is how your agricultural visual data is managed:

  • Storage Paths: Uploaded images are securely stored on our server media directories (media/) and linked to your private user account database records.
  • Spatial Vector Processing: Our neural network converts uploaded image pixel matrices into normalized numerical tensors for disease class classification and matching.
  • Diagnostic History: We retain your uploaded photos and predicted diagnostic tags so you can track crop disease progress and treatment history over time through your interactive farmer dashboard.
  • Model Training: Crop images may be anonymized and used to refine, validate, and retrain our deep learning model parameters, enhancing diagnostic accuracy for all farmers globally. No personal identifier is ever bundled with images during training.
3. Account Registration & Email Privacy

When you sign up to track your farm diagnostics, we collect basic registration data:

Account Security

User passwords are encrypted securely using Django's default PBKDF2 hashing algorithm with a SHA256 signature. We never store raw passwords on our servers.

Email Safeguards

We collect your registered email exclusively for secure authentication and diagnostic alerts. We do not rent, sell, or share email addresses with commercial advertisers.

4. Data Security and Governance

We implement rigorous security controls to guard your farm data:

  • Encrypted transport channels (HTTPS) secure all images, camera blobs, and credentials from mobile phone browsers to our database.
  • Strict access controls prevent unauthorized staff or external systems from accessing your specific farm diagnostics history.
  • You can request full deletion of your user profile, account details, and all diagnostic photos at any time by contacting our support team.
