Supported Diseases

Our advanced neural network classifies and recommends treatment strategies for multiple disease profiles.

Black Sigatoka
High

Necrotic stripes and dark spots causing leaf decay and fruit delay.

Yellow Sigatoka
Moderate

Fungal spotting spreading as yellowish oval lesions with gray centers.

Moko Disease
Critical

Bacterial vascular collapse showing yellowing, wilting, and stem decay.

Bunchy Top Virus
High

Viral bunching at pseudostem crowns causing stunted growth.

Cordana Leaf Spot
Moderate

Foliar spots displaying large oval brown sections with yellow halos.

Anthracnose
High

Dark rotting lesions spreading rapidly across mature crop segments.

Banana Mosaic Virus
High

Chlorotic bands and patterns resulting in reduced cellular strength.

Healthy Leaf
Optimal

Optimal green pigment and leaf surface area. No symptoms detected.
