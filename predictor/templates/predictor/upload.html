Tips for Farmers for Best Diagnosis
Good Lighting Take photos in bright day light. Avoid shadows or dark background settings.
Sharp Focus Keep your phone steady to prevent motion blur. The leaf texture must be clearly visible.
Isolate Leaf Isolate a single leaf sheet in the camera grid. Avoid capturing multiple layers of soil or trees.
Symptom Center Focus the camera lens right over the center of the spots, streaks, or wilting.
