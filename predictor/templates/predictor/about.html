About BananaGuard AI

Agritech deep learning banana crop diagnostic engine

Project Mission

BananaGuard AI is a high-fidelity web-based crop diagnostic engine designed to democratize high-accuracy plant pathology detection for farmers globally. By pairing standard mobile phone cameras with deep neural networks, BananaGuard delivers diagnostic results directly in the field.

Early leaf spot and pathogen identification can recover up to 50% of otherwise lost crop yields. BananaGuard closes the lag between symptom onset and crop advisor consulting by outputting instant, actionable biological treatments, prevention steps, and eco-friendly organic remedies.

AI Architecture
  • Core network: MobileNetV2 (pretrained on ImageNet)
  • Dual-Phase Transfer Learning framework
  • Custom deep classification head with Batch Normalization
  • Active fine-tuning of top 30 base layers
  • Target input scale: 224 × 224 pixels
  • Class weight balancing for low-represented disease photos
